Founded in 2011, EyeOn Networks benefits from the extensive experience of senior network consultant Robert DeUmberto, who has been in the industry since 1982. With roles ranging from field service technician to lead project consultant, Robert has worked with notable clients like Aurora Public Schools, Poudre School District, Fountain Fort- Carson School District, Colorado Golden Energy and Arkansas Valley Regional Medical Center.

Our mission is to build lasting relationships and become an extension of your team.

Our Goal:

EyeOn Networks offers professional network services across Colorado and is expanding to the Colorado Western Slope, Wyoming, Utah, Arizona, and New Mexico. From our Operations Center, we deliver both on-site and remote services tailored to project needs.

We cater to both large organizations and small to mid-sized businesses and school districts with limited resources. Our goal is to build long-term, trusting relationships, acting as an extension of your team. When you reach out, you’ll connect with a consultant already familiar with your network.
